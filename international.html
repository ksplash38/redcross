International Crating & Shipping

International shipping is a fast-paced, constantly evolving industry. New rules and regulations from a host of entities create challenges by governing what, where and how a business can ship commodities on a daily basis. Thailand Red Cross remains up to date on the latest laws governing the transportation of all types of goods. Our intensive training program and experience keep you updated on changes in the industry while keeping the supply chain in tact.
TRC’s involvement in various trade organizations such as the Transportation Intermediary Association (TIA) and several other networks which links us to many service providers throughout the globe. Thai International Shipping Line is happy to help you navigate your way through the world of international shipping.
We offer the following complementary services for International Shipping:

Ocean Freight Services

We ship full and partially-full 20’ and 40’ containers on freighters around the world. The following services are also available for Ocean freight service:
Global Supply Chain Management: TRC emphasizes the process of planning, implementing, and controlling the operations of the supply chain in order to optimize efficiency.
Insurance: We provide an insurance review tailored to your shipping needs.
Full Documentation Service: We will provide all documentation necessary for compliance around the globe and implement delivery.
Customs Brokerage: TRC prepares all documentation for export and import around the globe, keeping you in compliance with all customs laws.

Air Freight Services

Qualified products can be shipped around the world by passenger or cargo only freight planes according to Transportation Safety Administration (TSA) regulations.
The following services are also available for Air freight service:
Global Supply Chain Management: TRC emphasizes the process of planning, implementing, and controlling the operations of the supply chain in order to optimize efficiency.
Insurance: We provide an insurance review tailored to your shipping needs.
Full Documentation Service: We will provide all documentation necessary for compliance around the globe and implement delivery.
Customs Brokerage: TRC prepares all documentation for export and import around the globe, keeping you in compliance with all customs laws.

International Crating & Packaging-ISPM15 Compliant

We are fully compliant in handling all crating needs for both International and Domestic shipping! We are compliant with ISPM15 – where we are able to produce packaging that complies with all requirements pertaining to International Standards for Phytosanitary Measures of Guidelines for Regulating Wood Packaging Material in International Trade (ISPM15).
